Backyard Home (ADU) Feasibility Reports

Homeowners learn in minutes whether their lot can legally and physically hold a rental unit — before paying an architect.

Consumer reports / lead-genReal estate / housingfor Homeowners in ADU-friendly states (CA, WA, OR, CO...)ADU legalization wave

The idea

Problem

ADU rules vary by city, lot, zoning overlay, setbacks, and utility access. Homeowners can't tell if their backyard qualifies without hiring professionals, so most never start — and the ones who do often pay for feasibility work that returns a 'no'.

Solution

Address-in, report-out: parcel data + local zoning rules + setback/slope analysis produce a feasibility report with buildable envelope, unit size allowed, estimated cost range, and rental income projection. Sell the report, then refer qualified leads to local ADU builders.

The wedge: One metro with clean parcel data and strong ADU demand; the report is the lead magnet for builder referral fees.
