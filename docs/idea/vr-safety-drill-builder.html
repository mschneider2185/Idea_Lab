VR Safety Drill Builder for High-Risk Crews “DryrunVR”

Self-serve platform that converts a factory's existing written SOPs into immersive VR safety drills — no $50K custom build.

B2B SaaSIndustrial safety / trainingfor Safety managers at 50-500 employee manufacturers, warehouses, contractorsAI + VR cost collapse

The idea

Problem

Mid-market industrial companies carry serious workplace-safety liability every shift, but training budgets only cover slideshows and sign-off sheets. Custom VR training starts around $50K per build; generic VR libraries feel like video games built around the wrong equipment. Safety managers choose between compliance theater and enterprise price tags.

Solution

Upload a confined-space entry procedure or lockout-tagout protocol; the system parses steps, hazards, and decision points, then assembles VR drills from pre-built asset libraries (machinery, PPE, electrical panels, warehouse environments) that mirror what workers actually walk past on shift. Floor managers author new exercises without code.

The wedge: One procedure type (lockout-tagout) for one vertical (mid-market manufacturing) where OSHA citations are common and painful.
